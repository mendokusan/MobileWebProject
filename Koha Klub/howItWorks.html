How can you help?


If you are a small cafe or business, and are throwing away a lot of food, you can apply to donate this waste to a food insecure home. Donations can include; Food items (preferably items that don’t expire in a sort period of time), utensils/storage (containers, cutlery etc), and feminine hygiene items.



If you don’t have any food to donate but still want to help, you can volunteer to help us out. This could include doing deliveries, organising events etc.

How can I recieve help?


If you are struggling with food insecurity, don’t stress, we are here to help! You can apply to get donations such as food items or feminine hygiene products. We will send this application to a food bank, and you will be later notified.
We will also provide you with further tips and sources that may help you whilst you wait for a response.
