an easy way to give back!

If you are a small cafe or business, and are throwing away a lot of food, you can apply to donate this waste to a food insecure home. Donations can include; Food items (preferably items that don’t expire in a sort period of time), utensils/storage (containers, cutlery etc), and feminine hygiene items.
